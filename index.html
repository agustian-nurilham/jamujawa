What Is JamuJawa?

Jamu is a term for traditional medicine from Indonesia. Herbal medicine is made from natural ingredients, in the form of parts of plants.

there are lots of kind herbs, such as kunyit asam, beras kencur, cabe puyang, pahitan, kunci suruh, kudu laos, uyup-uyup, and sinom and then continue.

Drinking JamuJawa every day is an effort to maintain the body's resistance, health maintenance, recovery, fitness, and even beauty.
